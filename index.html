👋 Hello! I'm Zineddine, a software engineer passionate about creating immersive experiences. I built this interactive 3D solar system to learn Three.js while sharing fascinating facts about our incredible universe. Ready to explore? Let's get started! 🚀

🌟 When you look at stars, you're seeing the past - That star's light took years to reach you, so you're literally looking back in time.

🏖️ There are more stars than grains of sand - More stars exist in the universe than all the sand on every beach on Earth.

🪐 A day on Venus is longer than its year - Venus takes longer to spin once than to orbit the Sun.

⭐ You're made of dead stars - Every atom in your body was cooked inside an exploding star billions of years ago.

🌧️ There's a planet where it rains glass sideways - HD 189733b has winds over 5,000 mph that whip glass rain horizontally.

☀️ The Sun makes up 99.8% of our solar system - All the planets, moons, and asteroids combined are only 0.2% of the total mass.

🔵 A year on Neptune is 165 Earth years - Neptune hasn't even completed one full orbit since it was discovered in 1846.
